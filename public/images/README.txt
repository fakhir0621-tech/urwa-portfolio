=== HOW TO ADD YOUR PHOTOS ===

1. HERO PHOTO (circular photo on homepage):
   → Rename your photo to: hero.jpg
   → Place it in this folder: public/images/hero.jpg

2. ABOUT PHOTO (rectangular photo in About section):
   → Rename your photo to: about.jpg
   → Place it in this folder: public/images/about.jpg

Both photos should ideally be:
- Portrait orientation (taller than wide)
- Good quality (at least 500x600px)
- JPG or PNG format (if PNG, rename the file extension too)
